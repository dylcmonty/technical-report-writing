# FND Strategic Thesis

## Retrieval Gist

What is FND's core position on why local agriculture underperforms, and what is the company's answer to it?

## Claim
My thesis is that local agriculture is not mainly constrained by demand. It is constrained by fragmented operations, weak supply visibility, procurement friction, and poor interoperability across the systems that farms, markets, and buyers already use.

My position is not that local agriculture needs better branding alone. It is that local agriculture needs better coordination, cleaner interfaces, and shared machine-readable visibility of supply, demand, timing, perishability, and commitments. Producers should be able to offer in-stock or expected supply from the same systems they already use to manage websites, inventory, POS, taxes, planning, and events.
