## Fragment

I am a builder interested in the point where creativity and practicality meet. My background spans embedded systems, CAN-bus communications, EMI/EMC testing, cloud-hosted Python services, data pipelines, and self-directed hardware and software projects. Through Fruitful Network Development and funded entrepreneurial research, I have spent time talking with farmers, co-ops, extension programs, small businesses, and community organizations in order to understand technical needs as they are actually lived. I tend to enter unfamiliar areas by learning what I do not yet know how to ask, which is why my work often pulls in ideas from ontology, self-organizing systems, hardware integration, and information design. I am most at home in environments built by creative people on a mission to solve real problems.
