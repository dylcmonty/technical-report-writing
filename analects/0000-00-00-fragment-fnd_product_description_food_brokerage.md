# Product description

Fruitful Network Development is building a technology-enabled food brokerage for local produce.

Grocers, food hubs, and institutions often want to buy more local food, but local supply is hard to use reliably. Farms are fragmented, availability is unclear, ordering is manual, volumes are inconsistent, and buyers default to centralized distributors because they reduce procurement friction.

FND gives farmers free or low-cost web and operations tools that make their supply easier to publish, structure, and coordinate. That creates the supply-side network. The commercial product is the buyer-side brokerage layer: grocers and food hubs get a clearer and more reliable way to source local produce across many farms.

The long-term product is procurement infrastructure for regional food systems. Farmer tools create supply visibility, structured data makes the supply brokerable, and buyers pay for coordination, procurement, and transaction execution.
