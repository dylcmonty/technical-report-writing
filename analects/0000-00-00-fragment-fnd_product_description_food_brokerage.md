# Product description

## Fragment

I am building Fruitful Network Development as a technology-enabled brokerage for local produce.

Grocers, food hubs, and institutions often want to buy more local food, but local supply is hard to use reliably. Farms are fragmented, availability is unclear, ordering is manual, volumes are inconsistent, and buyers default to centralized distributors because they reduce procurement friction.

I use free or low-cost farm-side tools to make supply easier to publish, structure, and coordinate. That creates the supply-side network. The commercial product is the buyer-side brokerage layer: buyers get a clearer and more reliable way to source local produce across many farms.
