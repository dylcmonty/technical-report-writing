# Farm income volatility is the focused operating axis

## Retrieval Gist

What operating axis should Phase I use to keep the problem measurable?

## Claim
My focused operating axis is farm income volatility. I am not trying to solve every problem in local agriculture at once. I am treating unstable sell-through and unstable planning as a measurable constraint on farm profitability, reinvestment, and long-term reliability.
## Boundaries
- This does not make consumer access or grocery margins unimportant.
- This only defines the primary axis for Phase I.
