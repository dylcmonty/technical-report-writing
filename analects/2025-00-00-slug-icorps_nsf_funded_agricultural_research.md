# Icorps Nsf Funded Agricultural Research

Through funded entrepreneurial research, I conducted more than forty structured interviews across farms, co-ops, extension programs, specialty grocers, and related organizations. That work helped me produce technical requirements, data-model concepts, and a USDA-aligned pilot grant proposal. It also gave me direct exposure to the operational realities of local agriculture, which changed how I think about what good technical infrastructure should look like.
