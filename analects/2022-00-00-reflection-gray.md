# Gray

Remembering the past self, full of fault, can
feel like a dream. A dream that I was a
beast. A moment I remember living, but
one that I wasn't coonscious for. There
are memories of what my senses observed,
but there are no recolections of being
that beast and reflecting on that beast.
That kind of dream, you wake up from, Then
give thanks for this dream, in that it was
a dream. You revear your suffisticated
awareness that exists in your present
metacognition. Before, it was reality, the
dream. Merely a beast with a limited
sense for reality, to survive, to not feel
bad, and to feel good. You are better
now. You see beyond the view of this
limited existance. But how can I know that
I have awakened? How can I know that I
am yet to become aware of what I am not
or what I do not live in knowing? What am
I missing? What injustices do I see-through,
harm that I cause, stagnation that I cling to?
If I live to exist in the mind of others, if
the impression of others of my self & actions
are to make me what I am; it's then that I
become the apicdemy of an object. And if it
is my intention that I consider law, that I consider
truth, as reality; it is everything outside my
self I objectify with the disposition of a lonly
god. I may be a beast and I may not, but
I refuse death in unchangingness as an object and
I refuse death in absolute righteousness as
a lonly god. I choose in intention. This reality isn't
- gray and it isn't black & white. It is black, and it
- is white, not a mosaic but everything all at once. I chose Intent.

To say my manner of thought is correct or
to say my action is just, then I choose death
in certanty. And if I surmise these as wrong
then I choose death as an object, and
reject the ever-present reality that the
product of all that was once was, is whats
now. I have intention, and I am because
of what What. I eat from the tree of life,
and I hope to ever wake from my dreams.

~2022
