# Customer and operator posture

## Use When

Use when I need to explain FND to farms, food organizations, or operators who care first about immediate practical value.

## Fragment

I use FND to help farms and local food organizations run the web and operations infrastructure they already need: hosting, domain and email support, account access, forms, inventory structures, and other day-to-day workflow tools.

That immediate work matters because it solves real operating problems before asking anyone to join a new procurement network. It gives people usable infrastructure now, and it gives me the live operational context needed to build better coordination tools later.
