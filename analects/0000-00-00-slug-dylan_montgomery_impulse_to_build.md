# Dylan Montgomery Impulse To Build

The deeper problem I want to solve is the sense of helplessness that comes with loneliness and a lack of ability. 
I want to answer that by creating things that make people more capable and by believing it is possible to build something greater than what currently exists. 
A lot of my work, across software, hardware, and community conversations, comes back to that impulse.
