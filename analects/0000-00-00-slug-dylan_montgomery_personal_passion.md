# Dylan Montgomery Personal Passion

I am extremely passionate about enabling people.
About implementing the sense of primordial truth in data such that a pursuit of truth platformed by and embodying a system is intuitive and second nature.
To lessen the barriers between the borders of the unknown and a person’s  will to be more and do more.
My goal is to bring into the world a means of eliminating obstinatance from the world only conditional to people's intention.
To eliminate that which prevents specialist and generalist alike from being functional in their true nature.
I really do believe in human beings and I dream of providing the tools for human beings to embrace life and live as themselves in the trust of the world.
I believe that this is possible through ontologically sound schema, similar to that of the alphabet and its enabling of metacognition and self organization. In short not just entropy of information but ambiguous synchronicity.
