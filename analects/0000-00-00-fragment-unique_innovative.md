# Unique Innovative

## Fragment

I am not trying to build just another marketplace or inventory app. I am trying to build a shared data layer that lets farms, buyers, and local food organizations work from the same usable supply picture without forcing everyone onto one closed system.

The innovation is not control over the network. It is the ability to make distributed local data easier to structure, interpret, and act on, so local food can compete with less waste and less operational friction.
