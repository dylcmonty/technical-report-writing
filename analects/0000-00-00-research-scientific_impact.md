# Research Scientific Impact

Please describe the potential scientific impact of your research if successful (1-2 sentences, 200 words max) *

If successful, this project would contribute a reusable open framework for studying and improving small-farm productivity as a coordination and information problem rather than only a yield problem. That would create a tractable basis for measuring how interoperability,
 demand visibility, and planning certainty affect waste, sell-through, and income stability in diversified agricultural systems.
