# Phase I pilot partner outreach

## Use When

Use when inviting an early partner to consider a short Phase I pilot or feasibility collaboration.

## Fragment

I am applying for a USDA SBIR Phase I grant to feasibility-test tools that strengthen community-based agriculture. If funded, I would pilot and evaluate an implementation with a small number of early partners, including services far below market value in exchange for feedback.

A Phase I pilot could involve mapping coordination workflows, testing planning or inventory-visibility tools, identifying digital system friction and benefits, and checking whether the technical design aligns with cooperative or community values.

This is meant to be exploratory, collaborative, and short-term, not a finished product pitch or a long-term contract.
