# FND and CVCC Relationship

## Retrieval Gist

How should the relationship between FND and CVCC be accurately described?

## Approach
I support CVCC with digital infrastructure and operational systems work beyond basic website service. My public model is open-source, no-vendor-lock-in, and ownership-retaining: clients keep control of domains, payment systems, and mailboxes.

I treat CVCC as part of the real-world environment where I am learning, building, and testing practical agricultural software and coordination infrastructure. In that sense, the CVCC work is not just website maintenance. It is practical operating infrastructure work around communication, payments, recordkeeping, and optional interoperability across local food networks.
