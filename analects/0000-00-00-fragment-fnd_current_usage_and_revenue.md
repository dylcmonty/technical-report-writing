# Current usage and revenue

FND has early managed-service revenue through a signed customer relationship with an agriculture-adjacent organization.

The current paid work includes managed web and infrastructure services such as hosting, domain and email support, account access, web-service infrastructure, and related tools. This is not yet brokerage or transaction revenue. It is the first paid wedge into the farm and local-food operations network.
