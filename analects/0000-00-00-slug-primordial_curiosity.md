# Primordial Curiosity

My background is a mix of self-motivated hardware and software projects. I have taught myself KiCad to prototype a handheld device, built a laminar flow hood out of curiosity, and introduced myself to C++ through Speeduino and real-time engine data. Every new problem I face, whether mechanical or computational, fuels a kind of primordial curiosity about the world and what might be created within it.
