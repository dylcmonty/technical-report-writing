# Two-Sided Feasibility Window

## Retrieval Gist

What is the minimum condition for transitional feasibility in the Phase I framing?

## Claim
Feasibility exists only where producer-side and buyer-side conditions overlap. Producers must be able to improve net position, while buyers must remain at least non-worse-off on cost and service thresholds relative to their comparator sourcing. If that overlap does not exist under bounded assumptions, the pathway is not feasible in current form, regardless of narrative appeal.
## Boundaries
- This does not claim full market transformation from one positive scenario.
- This does not treat buyer or producer interests as optional in the decision rule.
