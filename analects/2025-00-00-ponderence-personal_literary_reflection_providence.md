# Providence

How does one put one's self at the center of their own world, to lack faith in that they seek to empower themselves by observing evil. As if to combat evil alone by staying weary, but here we often loose our selves to feeling as though we're in competition with this evil. We relish in seeing something we now feel as though we are safer. However, we are not safer, we are not better. To keep watchful eyes toward evil, only makes it so eyes are not kept watchful toward god. Know doubt, that there is wisdom in the  saying: "to be as wise as a snake but as innocent as a dove". However, there is also a great foolishness that we can be hopeful and faithful while our minds are perpetually occupied by outcomes we wish to not come about. Anxiety, depression, hopelessness, are all symptoms of trying to imagine what misfortune our life's can become. We cannot prevent misfortune, with power or thoughtfulness, we can only imbue our minds and life's with hope. A hope for goodness outside our power and outside our selves.
