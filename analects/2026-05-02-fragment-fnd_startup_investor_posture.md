# Startup and investor posture

## Use When

Use when I need to explain FND in startup, YC, or investor settings.

## Fragment

I am building Fruitful Network Development as a technology-enabled brokerage for local produce.

The buyer-side problem is procurement reliability: local supply is fragmented, timing is inconsistent, inventory is hard to see, and grocers or food hubs often default to centralized distributors because they reduce transaction friction. I address that by using farm-side tools and structured data to make local supply more visible, more reliable, and easier to coordinate.

In this frame, farms are essential participants and beneficiaries, but recurring buyers are the clearer paying customers. The software and open-source tooling matter because they create supply participation. The business becomes valuable when that participation turns fragmented farm output into brokerable supply.

## Do Not Use When

Do not use this as the lead framing for academic, USDA, or scholarship audiences.
