# Improvisational Technical Work

I have considerable experience talking to small business owners about their technical needs through my entrepreneurial pursuits and by discussing technical subjects with a business focus in pitch competitions; both of which I greatly enjoy.
I also have a lot of practice jumping into unfamiliar technical areas and “figuring it out” as I go. When there’s a problem in front of me, I find a bottomless source of motivation. For example, some areas I’ve jumped into headfirst include: building my car’s engine control unit and adapting open-source software for its engine tuning; programming software for insurance agents to call Medicare and Medicaid dataset servers and transform the results into sales leads; and teaching myself how to use KiCAD and SolidWorks to create an interface prototype and its housing, as well as manage its battery charging and discharging. Each of these presented a new set of challenges that required different problem-solving approaches. Thanks to this unique experience, I believe I align most closely with the Forward Deployed Software Engineer role.
