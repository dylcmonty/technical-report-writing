# Open Standard Best Interface Model

Question: How can an open standard still support a durable business position?

Answer:
The model does not depend on owning the whole market; it depends on becoming the most trusted and usable implementation around an open standard. The strategic position is to keep the core extensible for community development while competing on quality of interface, reliability of tooling, and clarity of coordination. In this structure, openness drives adoption and ecosystem growth, while implementation excellence drives authority and commercial durability.

Boundaries:
- This does not claim open standards automatically produce adoption.
- This does not claim brand trust can replace product quality.
