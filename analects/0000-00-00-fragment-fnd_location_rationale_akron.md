# Akron location rationale

Akron is the right initial base for FND because the first customer network is in Northeast Ohio.

The product depends on close access to farms, food hubs, local food organizations, and regional grocery buyers. Akron keeps operating costs low while the company builds from real deployments instead of abstract market research. Northeast Ohio also provides a practical testing ground for local-produce coordination because farms, local food organizations, and urban demand are geographically close enough to make adjacency part of the operating model.
