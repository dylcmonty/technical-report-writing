# Phase I scope guidance request

## Use When

Use when asking a program lead or sponsor for brief guidance on Phase I fit and scope.

## Fragment

I am preparing a Phase I proposal focused on feasibility testing, and before finalizing the approach I want to confirm that my interpretation of the topic objectives and Phase I scope aligns with program expectations.

If possible, I would appreciate a brief conversation to discuss how reviewers distinguish a true Phase I feasibility study from work better suited to Phase II, whether the proposal should emphasize prototype validation, pilot deployment, or evaluation design, and what kinds of coordination or network-oriented tools are usually treated as in scope.
