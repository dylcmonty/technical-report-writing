  - December 2025: $0

The current highest-paying work is contracted software/data-interface consulting for an Ohio House of Representatives campaign.

FND also has agriculture-adjacent managed web and operations-services usage, including hosting, domain/email support, account access, web-service infrastructure, and related operational tools.

## Interpretation

The revenue should not be represented as only agriculture revenue. The accurate frame is mixed early service revenue across:

1. agriculture-adjacent managed web/operations services;
2. non-agriculture data-interface consulting.

The non-agriculture campaign work should not be hidden, because it validates a transferable technical capability: ingesting, structuring, normalizing, and operationalizing fragmented domain data.

## YC posture

Current revenue is early and service-based. The important signal is not scale yet; it is that users are paying for the underlying capabilities FND needs for agriculture procurement: managed infrastructure, operational tooling, and structured data interfaces.

## Boundary rule

Do not call current revenue brokerage revenue. Brokerage, transaction fees, logistics coordination, and buyer procurement contracts are future commercial layers.
