# Leadership And Involvement Zips Electric

## Fact

With Zips Electric Racing, I served as drivetrain and ECU team lead and worked on the design and integration of the dashboard for the team’s electric vehicle. The role demanded hardware-software coordination, debugging, and close collaboration with other engineering students. It was one of the environments that pushed me to treat engineering as something built through iteration, ownership, and problem-solving under constraint.
