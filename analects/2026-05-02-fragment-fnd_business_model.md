# Business model

I monetize buyer-side coordination and brokerage while keeping farmer participation low-friction.

My first revenue comes from managed web and operations services for farms and agricultural organizations. That creates relationships and supply-side participation.

The scalable model is buyer-side: grocers, food hubs, institutions, and other recurring local-food buyers can pay for procurement coordination, brokerage, logistics coordination, and transaction execution.
