# Coordination burden is the immediate failure mode

## Retrieval Gist

What concrete failure mode am I targeting?

## Claim
The immediate failure mode is coordination burden. Local producers and buyers may both have reason to transact, but they still fail to coordinate efficiently because ordering, timing, volume, logistics, and information are fragmented. The weakness is not only production. It is the infrastructure that connects production to use.
## Boundaries
- This is narrower than saying local agriculture fails because of scale alone.
- This does not yet specify a single software feature.
