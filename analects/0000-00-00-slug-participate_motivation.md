# Participate Motivation

Why do you want to participate in this course?
The AgCorps I‑Corps Regional Course provides the perfect environment to move our technology from prototype to product‑market fit. While the core MSS parser and FND modules are already built, we recognize that the key to commercialization lies in talking to customers and iterating based on their needs. The Mycite paper underscores that the challenge for small agriculture is not technological limitation but a lack of discoverability and schedulability. Attending the American Farm Bureau Federation Convention will allow us to interview farmers, food‑hub operators and buyers from across the country to validate whether our assumptions about pain points and value propositions are accurate. We also hope to refine our customer segments, pricing model and go‑to‑market strategy based on real feedback.
The I‑Corps program's emphasis on customer discovery and evidence‑based entrepreneurship aligns with our goal of building a sustainable business around an open‑source core. We want to learn from experienced instructors and peers how to prioritize features, measure impact and navigate regulatory and IP considerations. Finally, participating in the course will help us build relationships with early adopters, potential partners and mentors in the agriculture sector, setting the stage for future NSF funding and scaling. In short, AgCorps offers both the training and the customer access we need to turn a promising technological framework into a viable commercial solution.
