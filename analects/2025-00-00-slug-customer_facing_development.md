# Customer Facing Development

When the weather is nice, I ride my bike around my county and talk to small business owners so I can understand their technical needs firsthand. That same instinct has carried me into conversations with farmers, market organizers, and community leaders. Those interactions keep my thinking grounded, because they show me that useful technology has to answer to actual people, actual workflows, and actual constraints.
