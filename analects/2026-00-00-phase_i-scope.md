# Phase I Scope

Phase I uses capacity bands, not agronomic optimization
Slug: boundary-phase-one-uses-capacity-bands-not-agronomic-optimization
Kind: boundary
Question: What kind of model am I building in Phase I?
Answer:
I am not building a full agronomic optimization model. Phase I uses current outputs from farmers and bounded estimates of practical capacity based on acreage, crop plans, and known farm constraints. The model is meant to test coordination feasibility under realistic supply bands, not to claim perfect prediction of biological yield.
Boundaries:
- This avoids promising a yield science project.
- This treats farm-reported data and bounded planning assumptions as the core inputs.
