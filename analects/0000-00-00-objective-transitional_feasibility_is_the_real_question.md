# Transitional feasibility is the real Phase I question
Slug: objective-transitional-feasibility-is-the-real-question
Kind: objective
Question: What does success in Phase I actually mean?
Answer:
The real question is whether there is a transitional pathway from current local production patterns to a more coordinated local supply system. I am not trying to prove that full adoption already exists. I am trying to show whether observed farm supply, bounded capacity, and buyer demand can move into a region where coordination becomes economically self-reinforcing.
Boundaries:
- This is a path question, not a finished-state claim.
- This requires explicit time horizons and scenario assumptions.
