# If I Could Solve A Problem Today

## Text

If you could solve any problem that impacts your daily life, what would it be and how would you solve it?

If I could solve any problem that impacts my daily life, it would be finding a way to feel purposeful and forward-moving. As a student, I’ve aimed to solve this by connecting with people. When the weather’s nice, I ride my bike all over my county and talk to small business owners to form connections and to familiarize myself with their technical needs, since I hope one day to be an entrepreneur who provides small businesses with technical solutions.
I also try to address this problem by networking with alumni and members of the University of Akron hacking club to find people who have something they want to achieve. I connect most easily with people on a mission, and I want to feed into a culture of big dreams, as well as be around others who share those aspirations.
Lastly, I continue to work on a software project I started two years ago, striving to build on my efforts and technical knowledge. Most recently, I adapted it to help my dad with his business by automating ETL processes for Medicare and Medicaid datasets. Ultimately, the problem I want to solve is the sense of helplessness that comes with loneliness and a lack of ability. I want to overcome this by daring to believe in creating something greater than what currently exists. I want to keep that hope alive.
