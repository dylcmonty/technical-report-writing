# Fruitful Network Development — Company Description

## Retrieval Gist

What is Fruitful Network Development, and what does it do?

## Description
Fruitful Network Development is an Ohio agricultural technology company and engineering consulting firm. It operates in two layers. The first is the current business: web infrastructure, payments, domain and email setup, onboarding, and operational support for farms, markets, and community organizations. The second is the strategic direction: open-source, interoperability-first software and instrumentation intended to let producers manage operations and publish current or expected supply from the same underlying records. The company was founded by Dylan Montgomery, a University of Akron computer engineering and applied mathematics student, and developed through customer discovery with agricultural stakeholders, I-Corps competition work, collaboration with Ohio State Extension's Northeast Ohio Agricultural Innovation Office, and applied systems work with local farm and community organizations. FND is pursuing USDA SBIR Phase I work to prototype and validate the Mycite framework in the field.
## Boundaries
- FND is not a fully launched brokerage, trucking network, or national marketplace. Those are future operational directions contingent on feasibility, pilots, and commercialization milestones.
- Do not describe the longer-range coordination or API network as currently deployed unless separately announced.
