# FND Ownership Model

## Retrieval Gist

What is FND's stance on client ownership, and why does it matter?

## Approach
My operating model centers on retained ownership. I work through scoped implementation access rather than permanent control, and clients keep control of their domains, payment accounts, and mailboxes.

Current services include website hosting and development, domain and email integration, payment and POS setup, onboarding, analytics, reporting extensions, and optional hardware or instrumentation support. I keep hardware and software open source where control, portability, and interoperability matter. The intended outcome is dependable local provisioning that improves income stability, reduces waste, and preserves local control rather than deepening vendor lock-in.
