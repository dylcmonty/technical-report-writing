# Farmers received 15.9 cents of each domestic food dollar in 2023

## Retrieval Gist

What official figure best anchors the claim that post-farm costs dominate food competitiveness?

## Fact

In 2023, farmers received 15.9 cents of each dollar spent on domestically produced food. The remaining share went to post-farm marketing functions such as processing, transportation, wholesaling, retailing, food service, packaging, and related labor.

## Boundaries

- This is a national food-system figure, not a local-food-specific metric.
- It is a cost-structure anchor, not a direct measure of farm profitability.
