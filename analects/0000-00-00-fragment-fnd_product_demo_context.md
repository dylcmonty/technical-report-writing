# Product demo context

A useful FND product demonstration should show the farmer-side adoption wedge and the path toward brokerage. The demonstration should include managed web services, client access, operational data entry, structured farm or supply information, and how that information could become useful to a buyer, food hub, or procurement workflow. The purpose is not merely to show a working portal; but to show how ordinary farm-facing tools can produce structured supply visibility.
