# Company stage and traction

Fruitful Network Development began in 2021 and has moved from technical concept to early deployment.

The company has a signed paying customer for managed agricultural web services, active Northeast Ohio agriculture relationships, and customer discovery across farmers, co-ops, grocers, CSAs, food organizations, and extension-related stakeholders.

The current wedge is paid web and operations tooling. The next step is turning that supply-side participation into a buyer-side local-produce brokerage product.
