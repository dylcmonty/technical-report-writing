Dean of The Humanities          their title

Dear Mr. Griffin:             formal greeting with a colon not a comma

I am excited to see the opening for a tenure track position.  My background includes teaching and many other responsibilities including advising, scheduling, evaluating instructors, interviewing prospective employees, writing marketing materials, facilitating in-services, and writing Ohio Board of Regents proposals, just to name a few.  purpose of letter

In addition to the traditional academic world, I also worked as a consultant for Corporate College and Quality Data Management. In these positions, I demonstrated the ability to resolve a variety of issues by helping organizations improve their efficiency and effectiveness.  body paragraph 1

I also have a strong technical background in MS Word, various online class platforms, and Integrated Video Distance Learning. Please see the accompanying curriculum vitae for details of my experience and education.  body paragraph 2

 I can offer you the teaching skills and secondary responsibilities you expect.  I can be reached by phone or email to set up an interview.  Thank you for your time—I look forward to learning more about this opportunity!   formal closing paragraph

Sincerely,                closing statement

*space for signature


Daniel E. Zawacki             your full name

Enclosure: Resume            Let the reader know what you have sent, if anything.

You can use fonts like Times or Calibri in the 10-14 point range.

The letter should be one page.
