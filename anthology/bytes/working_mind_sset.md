# My Working Mindset
I am determined to be the change that I want to see in the world, and I have faith that Palantir is where I will find the people, purpose, and work that supports that change; in the world and myself. From what I have come to understand, Palantir fosters the very ecosystem that I once thought that I could only create by starting my own company. I want to think creatively for solutions that aren't obvious, grow as a leader and a technical thinker, and form relationships with driven, dynamically innovative people. Working at Palantir as an FDSE means I wouldn't need work alone to create what already exists. I could grow in new ways alongside other people, different and alike, but all on a mission.
The Palantir approach
Opportunity to creatively explore problems while still applying grit
Opportunity to almost self sacrificially devote myself to a mission that I believe is greater than my small existence. by learning as much as I can and throwing my feet to the fire to then go further.
