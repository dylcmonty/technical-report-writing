My volunteer work with Big Brothers Big Sisters is one of the clearest ways I try to help others develop confidence, technical skill, and hope for the future. I meet regularly with my mentee, help coordinate activities and outings, and support both academic interests and personal growth. What I value most in that work is the way consistent attention and trust can change how someone sees their own potential.
