My work with the Hackron club has been about helping create a collaborative environment where students can explore technical ideas, discuss new tools, and build with other people who care about making things. I am drawn to spaces like that because I connect most easily with people on a mission, and I want to contribute to cultures that make technical ambition feel shared and real.
