What are your weaknesses?
I would say my weaknesses are rooted in my awareness of self deception.
I have a strong aversion to living a life that I try to make the world revolve around me. So if I meet self resistance, I often take it as feedback that I’m not meant to be doing that thing or in that manner. But I’m hyper vigilant to not let myself use this as a way to not work hard or be lazy. These two ideas can really take away from a sense of presence and calm determination, at least for long periods of time, without reassessing my thought pattern.
