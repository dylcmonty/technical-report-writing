# Learning What I Didn't Know

One thing I am proud of is my work on my software project's data schema. I am proud not just of how it has evolved, but of the multidisciplinary research it pushed me into. I was trying to learn what I didn't know I didn't know while developing the code and schema. That led me into ideas like NP-complete problems, ontology, and self-organizing systems, often by following conversations with professors and books that seemed unrelated at first. I value that kind of learning because it changes how I see the problem, not just how I implement the solution.
