At Stanley Black & Decker, I worked on electrical systems for next-generation equipment prototypes. I developed and integrated CAN-bus communications, supported motor and battery-management work, analyzed sensor-module data, and executed EMI and EMC testing with accompanying reports. That experience sharpened my interest in systems that have to be both technically precise and operationally reliable.
