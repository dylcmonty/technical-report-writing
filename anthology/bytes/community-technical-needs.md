# Community Technical Needs

When the weather is nice, I ride my bike all over my county and talk to small business owners to form connections and to familiarize myself with their technical needs. I hope one day to be an entrepreneur who provides small businesses with technical solutions. That same instinct has also carried me into conversations with farmers, market organizers, and community leaders, where I have seen how operational problems, data management, and local constraints shape what good technology actually looks like.
