# Primordial Curiosity

My background is a mix of hands-on, self-motivated projects. I have taught myself KiCad to design a single-hand held device inspired by historical tools like sextants and bows. I have built a laminar flow hood out of curiosity and a fascination with processes that harness nature. I introduced myself to C++ through Speeduino and real-time engine data. Every new problem I face, whether mechanical or computational, fuels a kind of primordial curiosity about the world and what might be created within it.
