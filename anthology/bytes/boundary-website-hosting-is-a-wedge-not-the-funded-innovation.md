# Website hosting is a wedge, not the funded innovation
Slug: boundary-website-hosting-is-a-wedge-not-the-funded-innovation
Kind: boundary
Question: How should I treat the website-hosting part of the business in relation to the proposal?
Answer:
Website hosting may be part of how the business enters relationships with farms, but it should not be confused with the funded innovation in Phase I. The proposal needs a clean distinction between the commercial wedge that gets users in the door and the technical uncertainty the research is actually resolving.
Boundaries:
- This avoids product conflation.
- This keeps the proposal centered on the feasibility question.
