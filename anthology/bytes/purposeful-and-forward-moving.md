A lot of what drives me is the desire to feel purposeful and forward-moving. I want to be in a culture of creative, smart people on a mission to solve problems, even if that means I have to help build that environment myself. I connect most easily with people who are trying to make something real, and I want my work to stay close to that kind of energy.
