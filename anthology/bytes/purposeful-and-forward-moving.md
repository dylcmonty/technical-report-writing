# Purposeful and Forward-Moving

I want to be in a culture of creative, smart people on a mission to solve problems, even if that means I have to build that environment myself. A lot of what drives me is the desire to feel purposeful and forward-moving. I solve that by connecting with people, especially people who are trying to build something real. I connect most easily with people on a mission, and I want to feed into a culture of big dreams while staying close to the work required to make those dreams real.
