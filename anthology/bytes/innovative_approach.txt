How is your approach innovative or unique (Max 150 words)?
Fruitful Network Development isn’t just building another marketplace or inventory app. It's creating a foundational data framework that allows any individual to operate software to manage digital resources in one place. Personal websites, inventory, onsite monitoring software. With all information in one place, a network is made to access information within a single business, as well as for suppliers and buyers to connect without a middle man. The innovation isn’t in controlling the network. It’s in empowering communities to self-organize, surface critical information, and unlock new ways to work together, without needing a central authority. Currently grocery stores and other customer end experiences are not able to utalize local agriculture as they are not a central point of contact, however, by allowing a full utalization of available information that already exists to help plan farming workflow, a local food system is able to operate at the same economies of scale with less waste and less harmful chemicals.
